About Bilinci

Bilinci is a real-time classroom translation solution designed for K-12 students. Our mission is to bridge language gaps in education and help every student succeed, regardless of language barriers. With Bilinci, classrooms become more inclusive, allowing ESL students to understand lessons in real-time and actively participate in learning.
