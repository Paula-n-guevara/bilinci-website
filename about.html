About Bilinci

Bilinci was created by a former ESL student-turned-teacher-turned-engineer. Inspired by a need for scalable classroom translation solutions, Bilinci bridges language gaps in real-time. When I taught game development in NYC public schools, I saw students disengaged simply because they couldn’t understand the lessons. That moment inspired me to build Bilinci – a solution powered by empathy and advanced AI.
